Centering Thoughts - Landslide

Let's get into a comfortable seated position. Once you've found your seat, set your plan for the day aside for the next 60 minutes.

Close your eyes, start to deepen your breath. Maybe start to roll your neck and shoulders. Start to become more aware of how you feel.

Lets move into our deep belly breathing, by taking three deep inhales, and exhales in through the nose and out through the nose. Becoming more aware of your breathing and heart rate.

After you take your last deep belly breath, lets move into our yogic breath, Ujai breathing. This is breathing in and out through the nose, keeping the mouth closed. Were going to practice three breaths together, try keep this breath throughout class as it helps to keep you cooler.


As we move into child's pose, encourage and remind yourself why you priortized this class today, and challenge yourself to give your body whatever it needs. That might be taking more breaks today.

Standing Series - Island, Dont Dream

CRESENT MOON R/L, Backward Bend, Forward Fold

Backward Bend - Hands in back pockets, legs engaged, hips pushed forward, exhale look back, go back, pushing hips forwads, try seeing where back wall meets floor. While keeping hips pushed forward slowly bring head up, tucking chin last, then folding forward.

Forward Fold - Take your feet hip width apart, lift your toes so you can slide your palms face up under each foot all the way to wrist crease. Elbows pointing in opposite directions, trying pushing legs to straight, pulling elbows to opposite sides of theroom, bringing forhead to shins.
