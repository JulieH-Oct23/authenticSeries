Meditative/Centering Thoughts

CHILDS POSE - Let's begin in Child's pose. That is big toes together, knees open as wide as the mat. Arms reachout in front of you, forehead to the mat.

Gently close your eyes. Allow your breathing to relax. As you inhale through your nose, feel your abdomen expanding out. As you exhale, allow the belly to fall between the thighs. PAUSE

With every inhalation, consciously slow your breath, bringing awareness to the space around your nostrils. With every exhale let go of all your thoughts or any external distractions. PAUSE

Observe the state of your mind. Set an intention for your practice today. Maybe its a commitment to priortize the breathwork over the postures. Make a resolution to practice this intention on your yoga mat today and through the rest of your day.

Continue taking deep belly breaths in through the nose, and out through the mouth. Work with the body you have brought to the mat today. Let go of all your responsibilities, tasks and to-do lists, as you find this deeper state of inner over the next 60 minutes. PAUSE

Become softer…soften your shoulders… jaw… face and allow your tounge to fall to the roof of your mouth. Let's move into our Ujayi breathing, close the mouth and breathing in and out through the nose.

On your next inhale, lets move into tabletop position.

Standing & Balancing Series - Section 2

Timing: Section ends 30 minutes into class.

CRESENT MOON R/L, Backward Bend, Forward Fold

Backward Bend - Hands in back pockets, legs engaged, hips pushed forward, exhale look back, go back, pushing hips forwads, try seeing where back wall meets floor. While keeping hips pushed forward slowly bring head up, tucking chin last, then folding forward.

Forward Fold - Take your feet hip width apart, lift your toes so you can slide your palms face up under each foot all the way to wrist crease. Elbows pointing in opposite directions, trying pushing legs to straight, pulling elbows to opposite sides of theroom, bringing forhead to shins.
